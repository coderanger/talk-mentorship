Interested in being Taught/Mentored?

No matter who you are, what programming language you use, or conference/ usergroup you want to present at or attend, you are welcome - you are our mission. We want to help.

Our Mentors make themselves available in a variety of ways it might be as little as answering questions on the mailing list, or IRC. Or they might do Skype chats with people in need of help coaching, lead or participate in local meetups, Google+ Hangouts or other areas.

Want to sign up?

It's simple! Subscribe to the list, and send an introduction email including a brief bio, experience, and what you would like help on.
